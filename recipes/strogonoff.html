Strogonoff

Description

Strogonoff is the only good thing created by the orcs. Even so, the brazilian version is much better, because mix the flavor of the ketchup with chicken and the soft of milk. This plate is accompanied by rice and french fries.

Ingredients

  • 200g of chicken
  • milk cream
  • Ketchup

Steps

  1. Cut the chicken in cubes
  2. season to taste
  3. Fry the chicken
  4. Add the milk cream
  5. Add the ketchup until the cream get pink
  6. serve with chicken and fries
